Catalogue of Human Rights Violations

This digital catalogue presents documented human rights violations in Liberia's recent history. The aim is to preserve memory, raise awareness, and support future education and accountability.

The material is thematically organized and includes both witness accounts and summaries of events related to massacres, imprisonment, sexual violence, and more.

Each entry represents a small part of a larger history that deserves to be seen, read, and remembered.
